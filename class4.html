CLASS FOUR

We leart how to use kinect and EinScan Pro to scan and edit body and ohter items in this class.

First, we use Kinect and Skanect software to make a scan of our body and export a STL model.

And we use meshmixer to repair the model.

We can also do some interesting edit on the model.

Then we use Einscan Pro to scan smaller items.

EinScan Pro is more sophisticated than kinect, but it can only scan light-colored items and it requires suitable light.

After scanning, we get two models, and we use Rhinoceros to put them together and make some exciting work.
